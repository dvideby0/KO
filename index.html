PWN The Future

What is the KnockOut? It's when fearless contenders power through one sleepless night to unleash limitless potential for credit unions everywhere.

Our 2nd annual 24-hour, no holds barred, Red Bull fueled innovation free-for-all takes place at PSCU's headquarters in St. Petersburg, Florida, September 26-27. From 10:00 a.m. on Thursday to 10:00 a.m. on Friday, bleary-eyed teams of five from across the country collaborate with each other and industry experts to pwn the future of payments.

Get your team together and get ready. It ain't gonna be easy.

Win Big

Big ideas can lead to big returns, and the KnockOut's no exception.

Our judges will choose three finalists. PSCU and participant credit union employees will vote to rank our champions. Winning team members will each receive the following:

1st Place: $1,500 Dream Vacation

2nd Place: Smart TV + Netflix

3rd Place: $250 gift card



Pineapple Prizes
Last year, Team Pineapple was our stand-out collaboration king. They openly shared ideas, helped other teams, and had a true credit union co-op spirit. To honor their spirit, we are giving away fantastic Pineapple Prizes to teams who are open, helpful, and collaborative.

There will also be great swag and giveaways throughout the night, so everyone goes home a winner.

Wanna Be A Contender?

Wanna compete? Wanna win? Well, there are a few rules you'll need to know about first.

First, you have to be an employee of PSCU or one of our Member-Owner Credit Unions to be in the KnockOut.

Second, you want to be part of a team of five. You'll want big thinkers, nerdy inventors, and smooth operators who can carry your idea from tech to tell in 24 hours. Your aim? Present a prototype to the judges that demonstrates function, feasibility, industry impact, time to market, cost, and ROI.

Is your team short of the magic number? Don't sweat it. We'll assign participants to join your team. Don't have a team? Email us. We'll assign you to a group that's short a member or two.

Third, all your development happens at KnockOut. Sure, you can brainstorm, have an idea in your back pocket, and start pulling your team together. But no keystrokes, plans, or prototype progress commences before the event. We want you to sweat out your best innovation that night.
